The Capsule Pyrale

How it works

Hermetic Sealing

The cover is lowered and locked with a special key, creating a completely airtight barrier — no leaks, no odors, no infiltration possible.

Inert Gas Preservation

The capsule is filled with nitrogen gas, naturally drying the body without chemicals or toxic release. Preservation lasts up to 100 years.

Gentle Placement

The body is placed by qualified professionals. Every gesture is slow, deliberate, and respectful — no rushed movements, no preparation or drying.

About Eterna

Frequently asked questions

Once the Capsule Pyrale is hermetically sealed, it is filled with inert nitrogen gas. Nitrogen displaces the oxygen inside the capsule, eliminating the conditions that allow decomposition to occur. The gas naturally dries the body over time — a process that is entirely physical, with no chemical agents involved. The result is preservation of up to 60 to 100 years.
